Dog Years is an ongoing narrative project exploring the shared history of humans and dogs across thirty-five thousand years.

Each entry is a standalone, immersive web story, pairing prose with custom visuals and soundscapes to reconstruct worlds that are goneâ€¦ or still arriving.

The stories range from prehistoric caves to medieval halls, imperial cities, research stations at the edge of the ice, and futures that might feel uncomfortably close.

Some are intimate. Some are strange. Some are quietly funny.

All are concerned with what it means to live alongside another mind.

Taken together, they trace a long companionship through violence, domesticity, joy, faith, play, boredom, collapse, and return.

Some will wag. Some may howl. A few may bite.
