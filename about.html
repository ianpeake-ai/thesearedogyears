Ian C Peake is a doctor, a former teacher, and always an ecologist. He lives in rural Scotland with his partner, a black Labrador called Echo, various fowl, and two cats who tolerate them all.

He writes about partnership, witness, and the long way round.

His weekdays belong to the city, most other times he can be found outside where the animals are.

Thank you for reading.
